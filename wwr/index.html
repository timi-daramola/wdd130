Gauteng

Gauteng is one of South Africa's nine provinces. Johannesburg the city of gold is one of its metropolis cities. It has Pretoria as the country's administrative capital. Johannesburg is a city with scenic beauty that has the country’s tallest skyscrapers. It houses the 17th Largest Stock Exchange in the world and the Largest Stock Exchange in Africa. Gauteng is South Africa’s economic powerhouse. The province serves as the center of finance, manufacturing, trade, personal services, government, transport, and others. It is bordered by North West Province to the west, Limpopo to the north, Mpumalanga to the east, and the Free State to the south.
